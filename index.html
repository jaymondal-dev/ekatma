Ekātma

एकात्मा • /'eːˈkaːt̪.ma'/ • One Self, unified soul

A journey into Advaita Vedanta and the eternal wisdom of the Self.

मनोबुद्ध्यहङ्कार चित्तानि नाहं
न च श्रोत्रजिह्वे न च घ्राणनेत्रे ।
न च व्योम भूमिर्न तेजो न वायुः
चिदानन्दरूपः शिवोऽहम् शिवोऽहम् ॥१॥

I am not the mind, intellect, ego, or memory,
Neither the ears, tongue, nose, nor eyes.
I am not space, earth, fire, water, or air -
I am Consciousness-Bliss, I am Shiva, I am Shiva.

- Nirvana Shatkam, Adi Shankaracharya

About

Welcome to Ekatma, a space dedicated to exploring the profound teachings of Advaita Vedanta - the philosophy of non-duality. Here, we delve into the timeless wisdom that reveals our true nature as pure consciousness, beyond the limitations of body, mind, and individual identity.

Through careful study of classical texts, contemplation of ancient verses, and practical inquiry into the nature of the Self, we embark on the most essential journey - the journey home to our own true nature.
