It hosts Sunday lectures, daily meditation and Vesper services, weekday scripture classes, plus retreats and festivals‚Äîthough summer classes are currently on break until September. 02. Arshabodha

A traditional Vedanta ashram in Somerset, NJ, founded by Swami Tadatmananda (disciple of Swami Dayananda), offering guided meditation, Vedanta & Gita classes, chanting, and outreach program. The site shares updates on class schedules, audio/video teachings, and details about community services and donations . It also hosts a YouTube channel featuring his discourses and satsangs for students worldwide .
